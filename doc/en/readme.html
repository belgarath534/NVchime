NVChime

Customize your NVDA startup and exit sounds with a built-in pack or your own WAV file.

Recommended Setup

Go to NVDA Settings, General, and uncheck "Play sound when starting or exiting NVDA" so only your NVChime sound plays.

Features

  • Six built-in sounds: Classic Chime, Retro Beep, Soft Bell, Dramatic Hit, Horror Sting, Chill Tone
  • Use any WAV file from your computer
  • Separate startup and exit sounds
  • Adjustable startup delay
  • Preview sounds before saving

How to Use

  1. Open NVDA menu with NVDA+N
  2. Go to Preferences, then Settings
  3. Find the NVChime category
  4. Configure your sounds and hit OK
